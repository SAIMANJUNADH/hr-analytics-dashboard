# HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive HR Analytics dashboard built using Power BI. It focuses on analyzing employee data to uncover workforce patterns, understand employee distribution, and identify key factors influencing attrition. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making for HR teams.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee demographics and workforce composition  
- Identify patterns and trends related to employee attrition  
- Understand income distribution across job roles and age groups  
- Evaluate department-wise and gender-wise employee distribution  
- Provide actionable insights for improving employee retention  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Total Employees  
- Average Age  
- Total Attrition  
- Attrition Rate  
- Average Income  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
- Research & Development department has the highest number of employees  
- Employees who travel rarely form the majority of the workforce  
- Higher age groups tend to earn higher average income  
- Attrition rate highlights potential employee retention challenges  

---

## 📊 Visualizations Included
- Average Income by Job Role  
- Income Trend by Age  
- Employees by Department and Gender  
- Employees by Job Level  
- Business Travel Analysis  
- Age Group Distribution Table
